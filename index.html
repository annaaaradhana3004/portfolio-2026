The Challenge

Spatchcock Funk, an independent F&B media brand, needed a scalable, data-driven strategy to grow their audience, drive revenue, and identify the right technology to unify their marketing efforts.

Our Approach

Conducted audience segmentation identifying two core personas — Sophisticated Sam (45, Denver professional) and Creative Cara (28, Brooklyn creative). Mapped behavior, platform habits, and spending patterns to build a unified strategy.

The Recommendation

Recommended a Twilio Segment + Salesforce tech stack deployed across four phases over 18 months — from platform launch and A/B testing to live event activation and predictive sponsorship modeling.
